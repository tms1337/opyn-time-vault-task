Test Cases:

Normal Cases:
User withdraws after single deposit.
User withdraws after multiple deposits.
User withdraws after reward calculation.

Edge Cases:
Withdraw with zero deposits.
Withdraw immediately after deposit (minimal rewards).
Withdraw after partial reward pool depletion.

Multi-Interaction Cases:
Multiple users withdrawing simultaneously.
Users withdraw after deposits and owner adds more rewards.
Users withdraw, deposit again, then withdraw.
